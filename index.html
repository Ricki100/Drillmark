Welcome to DRILLMARK

Zimbabwes' Leading Distributors for highly specialized, bearings, seals, power transmission, mechanical and instrumental engineering spares and supplies.

We are suppliers of NIS bearings (Japan), IKC bearings (India); and stock some of the world’s top bearings brands such as SKF, NSK, NTN, RHP, TIMKEM, FAG, KOYO, MACGILL and INA.

About Drillmark

Drillmark bearings and Engineering supplies was incorporated in 2003 as a wholly indigenous provider of integrated engineering solutions as part of Drillmark Investments PL founded in 2003. The company started operations in January 2004 and over the years, we have managed to steadily grow to become a major player in the market.

The company is dedicated to being a truly representative Zimbabwean organization. We are a highly professional stockists and sourcing agents. Service was varied until 2006, when the company’s foundation was laid in the supply of products in the manufacturing, agricultural, mining and engineering sectors. A reflection of our loyalty and business integrity of Drillmark Bearings and Engineering Supplies, the company imports both stock and indent products from our network partners across the globe that enables us to maintain excellent pricing due to our discount structure with our national and international partners.
